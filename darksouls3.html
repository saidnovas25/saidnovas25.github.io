Dark souls 3

Dark Souls III is a 2016 action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco
Entertainment for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Windows.  The third and final entry in the Dark Souls series, players
have access to various weapons, armour, magic, and consumables that they can use to fight their enemies. Dark
Souls 3 has an amazing story and set pieces to explore, the game is diffucult at time but its diffuculty adds to the overall experience of playing a dark souls game. Personally Dark Souls 3 has easily became one of my favorite games of all time
and I have not even beaten it yet.
