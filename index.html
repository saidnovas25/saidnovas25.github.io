Video Games

Ive been playing video games since as long as I remembered. In the Dominican Republic my uncles who live in
the State's would send consoles and video games to me and my mom in the DR. The first console I owned was a
Nintendo Wii which my uncle sent from the US, the wii was the first time I started playing video games. Things like
Wii Sports, Super Smash Bros Brawl, and Super Mario. After that my mom and I moved to the United States which
introduced me into even more videos game since we lived at my grandma's house where my uncle lived at.

My uncle introduced me the PlayStation 3 console which was Relatively new at the time. I would see him play all day after he
came back from School and work. He would mainly play Call of Duty games which I should have not been watching but I sure did.
I instantly fell in love with the Series after seeing him and his friend play Online matches between each-other.

Here are some of my all time favorite games:

  • Call Of Duty Black Ops (1 & 2)
  • GTA 5
  • Destiny 2
